Adeola Model School, which is privately-owned and approved to run as a private Nursery & Primary School in 1967 was established by the Late Chief (Mrs.) Felicia Adeola Adesiyun; a mother and professional teacher with years of exceptional experience in Nigeria and the United Kingdom.


Prior to the approval, the Nursery class was opened in April, 1960, with a class of only four pupils and the first set of six pupils passed out in flying colors to various secondary schools on the 7th of December 1967. Ever since, the numbers of our students have increased considerably with over twenty-two classrooms and adequate members of staff.


On 24th June 1967, the then Military Governor of Kwara State, Lt Colonel D.L. Bamigboye visited the school and was very impressed with the rapid growth and quality of standard education provided by the school.


Ever since then, the school has maintained that of standard and improves the quality of education with well-trained teachers, modern classroom technologies and conducive environment for students to learn faster. The school now runs from Pre-nursery class to Primary 6 where pupils sit for the First School Leaving Examination for entry into Secondary Schools of their choice.
